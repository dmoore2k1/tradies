FAQ

Common questions.

No complicated packages. No fake “limited time” pressure.

Is this a full website?

It is a focused starter website, built around trust and enquiries instead of unnecessary pages. That is the point: fast to build, easy to understand, and low maintenance.

What does the $59/month cover?

Hosting, SSL, form delivery, basic uptime/keep-it-working checks and keeping the website online. Ongoing edits are quoted separately unless we add a higher care tier later.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. We can connect your existing domain or help you choose one if you do not have one yet.

What if I already have a site?

If your site is decent, we will not pitch you “you need a website”. You may be a better fit for an enquiry-path refresh.

Do you do logos?

Yes, as a simple Logo Reboot add-on. It is a cleanup or simple wordmark, not a full brand identity project.
